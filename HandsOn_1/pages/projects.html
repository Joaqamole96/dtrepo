Featured Projects

Here are some of the projects I'm working on recently. Click on any project to learn more about it!

UMatch

An online lost and found system designed for the University of Makati students and staff to report and recover lost items. It features location-based reporting, item categorization, and a user-friendly interface to streamline the process of reuniting owners with their belongings.

Acadenza

A college productivity app aimed at helping students manage their tasks, schedules, and collaborative activities. Currently in development, it features planning tools with future goals of becoming a shared student hub for group productivity.

Dungeon Frontier

A Unity 3D rogue-like action RPG mobile game where you play as a Sentinel, exploring an unstable dimension filled with incomprehensible creatures and untold treasures. The game makes use of various extensive algorithms such as prodecural generation and enemy pathfinding & positioning to maximize replayability and satisfaction from completing a playthrough.
